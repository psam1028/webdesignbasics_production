I'm a native New Mexican currently studying Culture + Media and Journalism + Design in New York City. My interests lie in archiving moments and phenomena that resonate with the individual and support complex narratives. Because media is a mirror into collective thought, I believe making sustainable media is the way to create dialogue. Through my work, I strive to decolonize our lives.
