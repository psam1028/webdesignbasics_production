CELINE

Celine is a French luxury brand. As part of my project, I set out to rebrand this company's image. Using Bauhaus themes and bold colors, the new image comes out very strong. The connection was to use Surrealism as part of the foundation for the company. With this, the theme of identity came into play, while also exploring Surrealism in fashion. Elsa Schiaparelli, an Italian fashion designer, was known to explore in the realm, and was one of the first designers incorporate Surrealism in fashion. For the new product line up, there will be a series of hats, inspired by the work of Elsa Schiaparelli.
