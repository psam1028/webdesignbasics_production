Artist Statement

Carsen Russell is a photographer and writer. She will receive a BFA of Photography from Parsons the New School of Design and a BA of Literary Studies: Creative Writing from Eugene Lang the New School for Liberal Arts. Her works deals with elements of fiction and the construction of a story, the relationship between the artist and viewer, the interaction of word and images, and the family narrative. Her writing and photography has been published in Seen Heard Known Magazine and shown at the Anna-Maria and Stephen Kellen Gallery in New York. She lives and works in New York City and Austin, Texas.

She can be contacted at carsenrussell@gmail.com.
