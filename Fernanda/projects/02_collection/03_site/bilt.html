Bilt Furniture was created with the idea of having a DIY furniture place where people would have all sorts of materials to buy, and all the machines necessary to create their own furnishings. Besides that, helpers would be available if customers had any questions about how to build their furniture. In other words, Bilt would have everything necessary for customers to make their own unique furnishings, avoiding the high prices and sameness of current furniture stores. To promote the idea, I created a marketing material.
