Joya Widney is a sophomore at Parsons the New School for Design. She is working towards a Bachelors of Fine Arts in Product Design with a minor in Communications Design. She has an extensive background in the arts. She took dance lessons since age two, began acting at age seven, art classes at age ten, vocal lessons at age twelve, and graduated high school from a charter high school specializing in the arts with a major in theatre. She has worked in set, lighting, costume and prop design for theatre as well as stage managing, directing and acting. She is a pro with the Adobe suite, especially Illustrator and Photoshop. She is beginning to learn how to code HTML/CSS and CAD She is proficient with Microsoft Office Suite, iWork and Google Docs.
